NetworkGeometryProcessor

This example shows how to plug NetworkGeometryProcessor into Aligner.

Why this processor

NetworkGeometryProcessor is generally slower than DieussaertGeometryProcessor, but it is more generic for network-like alignment scenarios and mixed geometry behavior.

What the script does

  1. Builds a processor with ProcessorConfig.
  2. Injects that processor into the Aligner.
  3. Loads one thematic geometry and GRB reference data.
  4. Runs prediction over multiple distances.
  5. Visualizes both the selected candidates and geometric change trend.

Practical guidance

  • Start with this processor only when your use case truly requires network behavior.
  • Keep your distance series small first; expand it after validating quality.
